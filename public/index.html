Answers for readers and AI search

EPUB to PDF questions, answered clearly.

What is Epub2Pdf?

Epub2Pdf is a web converter that turns EPUB books into readable PDF files for archiving, printing, and offline reading.

What file size can I upload?

The current limit is 30 MB per EPUB file. The converter validates the archive before starting heavier rendering work.

Are uploaded files kept permanently?

No. Uploads and temporary render folders are removed after conversion or failed requests.
